Fund Name: Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Growth

About the Fund:
Groww ELSS Tax Saver Fund Direct Growth is an Equity ELSS path scheme with a 3-year lock-in period. Launched by Groww Mutual Fund and available since 10 Apr 2008. The current fund managers are Paras Matalia and Nikhil Satam. The fund has an AUM of ₹50.88 Cr and the Latest NAV as of 02 Mar 2026 is ₹21.92. It is rated Very High risk. The benchmark is the BSE 500 Index.

Investment Objective:
The Scheme seeks to generate long-term capital appreciation from a diversified portfolio of predominantly equity and equity-related Securities. The scheme offers tax benefits under Section 80C of the Income Tax Act.
